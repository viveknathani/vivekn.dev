work

I build and maintain backend systems at Investmint. Here's a brief list of interesting things I have built in my time here so far:

  • inves-broker - A heavily used internal library written in Node.js for interacting with multiple broker APIs in the Indian stock market.
  • news feed - This is a real-time, AI powered news feed that sends the latest updates for all the companies who have their stocks listed on NSE.
  • portfolio import and analysis - You can import your holdings from 10+ brokers in India and get detailed analysis on top of it.

I believe technology is an accelerator for making education accessible. With the right intentions and decent capital at disposal, we can truly make a dent in the universe.
